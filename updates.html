New Updates in App

28 Jan, 2016

We have launching Daily Transformation, with a well-built Services of strong privacy principles in mind. In our updated Terms and Privacy Policy you'll find:

  • - Our updated Terms and Privacy Policy are easier to understand and reflect new features just like internet access for snapshot updating, caption box, mentioning the type of image,whether it is front view or side view and many more.

  • - Our Privacy Policy explains how we work together to improve our services and offerings, like fighting spam across apps, making product suggestions, and showing relevant offers and ads on Facebook. Nothing you share on Daily Transformation, including your photos, and account information, will be shared onto Facebook unless you permit it.

  • - Your snapshots are yours, and we can't read them. We've built privacy, and other security features into Daily Transformation. Your snapshots are now saved to a cloud server so that no one can access it illegally.

  • - If you are an existing user, you can choose to have your Daily Transformation account information shared with Facebook to invite your Facebook friends to learn about this app. Existing users who accept our updated Terms and Privacy Policy will have an additional 30 days to make this choice by going to Settings > Account.
